White Water Rafting

The perfect balance between thrill and safety, rafting delivers an adrenaline-packed experience that’s still family-friendly. It’s more than just navigating rapids—it’s about creating unforgettable memories, overcoming challenges together, and strengthening bonds. With every paddle stroke, you’ll feel the rush of wild nature while trusting in a controlled, expertly managed environment. Rafting is an intense mix of emotions—excitement, connection, teamwork—where collaboration between friends or family becomes key to conquering the course. An adventure that begins on the water but lives on in the stories you’ll share long after.

History

"In 2005, a group of passionate rafting guides—led by the adventurous Marco Silva—founded Rafting Aventura with a single inflatable boat and a dream: to share the magic of Brazil’s untamed rivers. What began as weekend expeditions for friends soon turned into a mission to make rafting accessible to all, blending safety training with heart-pounding fun.

Today, we’re pioneers in eco-friendly adventure tourism, with over 15,000 satisfied adventurers guided down the Rio Jacaré’s rapids. Our certified instructors train Olympic athletes and families alike, but our philosophy remains the same: the river teaches teamwork, courage, and respect for nature’s raw power.
